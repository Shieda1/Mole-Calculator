What is the mole?
Mole is the unit of amount of substance in the international system of units. The amount of substance is a measure of how many elementary entities of a specific substance are in an object. The mole contains 6.02214076 * 10^23 elementary entities.
How to calculate mole?

Why use mole?
Chemists use the mole because atoms and other particles are very small and alot is needed in order to weight them.
